Mold Sampling That Actually Finds the Problem
Here's how mold sampling San Clemente homes actually works. I don’t just wave some device around and call it done. We do air sampling to see whats floating around that your breathing. We do surface sampling on walls, ceilings, and floors. We do cavity sampling inside walls if I think theres hidden growth.
The air sampling catches spores that are circulating through your HVAC system. You might have mold growing in one room, but the air conditioning is spreading it through your whole house. I see this a lot in homes where people close off rooms they dont use - the mold grows there, but everyone in the house is breathing it.
Surface sampling tells us what kind of mold your dealing with. Not all mold is the same. Some types are just annoying. Some types will make you sick. Some types will actually damage your house structure. You need to know which one your fighting.
For hidden mold, sometimes we gotta make a small inspection hole in the wall. I know that sounds scary, but its way better than letting mold spread for another year because you didnt want a tiny hole that can be patched in 10 minutes.
Every mold sampling in San Clemente gets sent to the same certified lab I've used for a decade. Results come back within 3 days. Then I sit down with you and explain what we found in plain English - not in technical terms that make no sense.
