## HVAC System Inspection

We focus on moisture, visible condition, and air quality red flags.

We typically inspect:
- Air handler / furnace cabinet
- Evaporator coil area
- Condensate drain line and pan
- Filter fit and bypass gaps
- Supply and return vents
- Attic ducting (if accessible)
- Obvious airflow imbalance

## Why HVAC matters for mold risk

- Sweating ductwork
- Poor filtration
