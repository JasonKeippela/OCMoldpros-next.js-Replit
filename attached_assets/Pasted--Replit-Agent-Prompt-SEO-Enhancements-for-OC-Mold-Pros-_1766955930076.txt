## 4. Optimize Image Alt Text

**Task**: Add descriptive, keyword-rich alt text to all images on the site.

**Implementation**:
- Review every image on the site
- Add alt text that describes the image AND includes relevant keywords naturally
- Ensure alt text is helpful for screen readers and SEO

**Alt text examples**:

- **Logo**: `alt="OC Mold Pros - Professional Mold Inspection Services in Orange County, CA"`
- **Hero image**: `alt="Professional mold inspector examining Orange County home with thermal imaging camera"`
- **Service icons**: 
  - `alt="Mold inspection icon - comprehensive visual inspection services"`
  - `alt="Air quality testing icon - professional mold spore analysis"`
  - `alt="Moisture detection icon - infrared thermal imaging technology"`
  - `alt="Mold inspection report icon - detailed findings and recommendations"`

**General rules**:
- Alt text should be 125 characters or less
- Be descriptive and specific
- Include location (Orange County) where relevant
- Include service type (mold inspection, testing, etc.)
- Don't keyword stuff - keep it natural
