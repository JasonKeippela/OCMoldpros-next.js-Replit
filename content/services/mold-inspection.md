# Mold Inspection San Clemente – finding the why, not just the mold

Seeing mold is one thing. Understanding why it's there is what actually fixes the problem.

A **mold inspection in San Clemente** goes beyond looking at surfaces. We trace the moisture source, assess the scope, and give you a clear picture of what's happening.

## Mold Inspection

A thorough mold inspection covers:
- Visual assessment of all accessible areas
- Moisture readings with professional meters
- Thermal imaging to find hidden moisture
- Air quality evaluation when appropriate
- HVAC system check for moisture concerns
- Exterior review for drainage and water management

We look at the whole house, not just the spot that caught your attention.

## Home Inspector

As a home inspector, we understand how buildings work. Mold is almost always a symptom of a moisture problem. Our job is to find that moisture problem.

We explain what we find in plain language and help you understand priorities.

## FAQ

### Who offers mold inspection near me in San Clemente?
We provide professional mold inspections throughout San Clemente and surrounding Orange County areas.

### How is this different from mold testing?
Inspection is the visual and physical assessment. Testing adds lab analysis of samples.

### Do you remove mold?
No. We inspect and document. We can refer trusted remediation professionals.

### How long does a mold inspection take?
Usually 1–3 hours depending on property size.

### What if you don't find mold?
That's good news. We'll still document any moisture risks.

### Should I get an inspection before buying a home?
Yes. It can reveal hidden issues that affect your decision.
