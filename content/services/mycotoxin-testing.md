# Mycotoxin Testing San Clemente – when symptoms don't add up

Sometimes mold testing shows elevated levels, but the real concern is what the mold is producing. Mycotoxins are toxic byproducts of certain mold species that can affect health.

**Mycotoxin testing in San Clemente** helps identify whether harmful mold byproducts are present in your indoor environment.

## Mycotoxin Testing

Mycotoxins are chemicals produced by mold. They can be found in:
- Dust settled on surfaces
- Air samples
- Building materials
- HVAC systems

Not all mold produces mycotoxins, and standard mold testing doesn't always detect them. Mycotoxin-specific testing fills that gap.

Common mycotoxins we test for include:
- Aflatoxins
- Ochratoxin A
- Trichothecenes (including satratoxin)
- Gliotoxin

### Is mycotoxin testing different from mold testing?
Yes. Mold testing identifies mold species. Mycotoxin testing identifies toxic byproducts.

### Do all molds produce mycotoxins?
No. Only certain species produce them under certain conditions.

### How long until I get results?

### Can mycotoxins be cleaned up?
Yes, but the source mold and moisture must be addressed first.
