## Mold Testing

Mold testing includes several methods depending on the situation:
- Air sampling to measure airborne spore concentrations
- Surface sampling to identify mold on materials
- Outdoor control samples for comparison
- Lab analysis with species identification

We choose the right combination based on what we find during the inspection.
