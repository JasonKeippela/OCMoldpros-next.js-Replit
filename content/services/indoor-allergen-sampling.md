## Indoor Allergen Sampling

Indoor allergen sampling tests for common airborne and settled allergens:
- Mold spores
- Dust mite allergens
- Pet dander
- Pollen particles
- Other particulate matter

We collect samples from air and surfaces, then compare results to established thresholds.

## Home Inspector

As a home inspector, we look beyond the lab results. We inspect conditions that contribute to allergen buildup:
- Ventilation quality
- Humidity levels
- HVAC filter condition
- Carpet and upholstery dust
- Moisture sources that feed mold

Understanding the environment helps explain the results.
