# Mold Remediation San Clemente – inspections protect the process

Mold remediation is the process of removing mold and restoring affected areas. Done right, it solves the problem. Done poorly, it wastes money and leaves risks behind.

**Mold remediation in San Clemente** should always start and end with inspection and testing. That's where we come in.

## Mold Remediation

We don't perform remediation. We provide the inspection and testing framework that protects the process.

Before remediation:
- We document the extent and location of mold
- We identify the moisture source
- We collect baseline air and surface samples
- We help define the scope of work

After remediation:
- We verify the work was completed properly
- We collect post-remediation samples
- We compare results to baseline
- We provide clearance documentation

This before-and-after approach protects homeowners and holds remediation contractors accountable.
