## Mold Cleaning

We don't perform mold cleaning or remediation ourselves. What we do is provide the inspection and testing that should happen before and after cleaning.

Why inspection matters before cleaning:
- Identifies the moisture source feeding the mold
- Determines if the problem is surface-level or deeper
- Helps decide if professional remediation is needed
- Creates baseline documentation

Why inspection matters after cleaning:
- Verifies the work was done properly
- Confirms airborne spore levels have returned to normal
- Provides documentation of completion
