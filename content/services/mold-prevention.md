# Mold Prevention San Clemente – stop it before it starts

Most mold problems don't start as mold. They start as moisture that gets ignored.

A **mold prevention inspection in San Clemente** focuses on finding moisture habits and fixing them early.

## Mold Prevention

We look for conditions that allow mold to grow:
- High indoor humidity
- Poor bathroom ventilation
- Small plumbing leaks
- Window condensation
- Attic and crawlspace moisture
- HVAC drain issues

## Home Inspector

A prevention-focused inspection checks:
- Bathrooms and fans
- Kitchen and laundry areas
- Attics and roof penetrations
- Crawlspaces (if accessible)
- Windows and sliders
- Exterior drainage and gutters
