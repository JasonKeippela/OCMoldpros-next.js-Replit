# Thermal Imaging Inspection San Clemente – see what the eye can't

Some moisture problems are invisible. Water behind walls, under floors, or above ceilings doesn't always leave obvious signs until damage is advanced.

A **thermal imaging inspection in San Clemente** uses infrared cameras to detect temperature differences that often indicate hidden moisture.

## Thermal Imaging Inspection

Infrared cameras don't see through walls. They detect surface temperature patterns that suggest what's happening behind them.

What thermal imaging can reveal:
- Moisture behind drywall
- Active leaks in plumbing or roofing
- Insulation gaps or missing sections
- HVAC duct leaks or condensation
- Areas of poor ventilation
- Water intrusion paths from exterior

We use thermal imaging as part of a complete inspection, not as a standalone tool.

## Home Inspector

As a home inspector, thermal imaging adds another layer of information. When we see a suspicious area visually, thermal imaging helps confirm or rule out moisture.

It reduces guesswork and helps target sampling or further investigation.
