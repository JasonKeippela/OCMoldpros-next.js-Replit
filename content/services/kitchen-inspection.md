# Kitchen Inspection San Clemente – where small leaks cause big problems

Kitchens hide a lot of plumbing. Slow leaks under sinks, dishwasher issues, and fridge water lines cause more damage than most people realize.

A **kitchen inspection in San Clemente** focuses on moisture, plumbing, and early warning signs before cabinets and floors get damaged.

## Kitchen Inspection

We inspect the common kitchen risk areas:
- Sink base cabinet and supply lines
- Drain connections and traps
- Dishwasher connections and drain lines
- Refrigerator water supply line
- Cabinet flooring for swelling or staining
- Wall and baseboard moisture signs
- Ventilation and humidity issues

These problems often go unnoticed until damage is already done.
